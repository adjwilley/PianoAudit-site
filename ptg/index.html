Android a few more steps

Google Play requires a payment method on file and a few extra steps:

  1. Download PianoAudit from the Google Play Store if you haven't already
  2. Open PianoAudit, tap Settings, then Upgrade
  3. Select the Pro subscription
  4. Tap the arrow (›) next to your payment method to change it
  5. Scroll down and tap Redeem code
  6. Enter the code: PTG2026
  7. Confirm it shows $0.00 or "Free for 90 days", then tap Subscribe
  8. Cancel the subscription to prevent auto-renewal (see below)
Important: Android subscriptions auto-renew after 90 days. Cancel anytime during your trial to avoid charges — you keep full access until the trial ends.

How to cancel (Android only)

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile picture (top right)
  3. Tap Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  4. Find PianoAudit, tap it, and tap Cancel subscription

Or on desktop: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions
