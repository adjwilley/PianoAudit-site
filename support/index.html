Support

Questions, problems, or ideas? Email support@pianoaudit.com and a human (the piano technician who makes the app) will get back to you.

Quick answers

Does the app record my piano?

No. Audio is analyzed live and immediately discarded — only the measured numbers are kept, on your device. See the privacy policy.

How many keys do I need to play?

The more the better. Around 20 keys across bass, middle, and treble is enough for a confident verdict; playing everything gives the fullest picture.

Is PianoAudit on Android?

Yes. PianoAudit is built for both iPhone and Android around the same measurement engine — same screens, same tiers, same reports.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Purchases belong to your Apple or Google account, not the device. Install PianoAudit under the same account, then open the upgrade screen (Plus & Pro) and tap Restore Purchases if anything hasn't unlocked.

How do I move my pianos to a new device?

Export each piano from the Pianos screen (long-press → Export Data), send the files to the new device, and use the import button at the top of the Pianos screen. Exports move freely between iPhone and Android — the file format is identical.

I'm a technician — how do customer reports work?

Measure before you tune, measure after, select both sessions and tap Compare, then Customer Report (PDF). Your business details go on the header the first time and are remembered. Share by email, text, AirDrop, or any share option on your phone.
